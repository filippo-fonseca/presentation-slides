## Theme

Dark pitch preset (`#0a0a0a` neumorphic). The brand soul is **deep forest green + a sharp copper accent** — botanical-meets-technical, premium and calm, never generic-startup. On the near-black base, lead with copper for accents and glow, and use forest/sage greens for secondary surfaces and tints so the deck still reads "field / living / growth."

### Colors

- accent: #B26A2B (copper — the one sharp accent; use for glow, key phrases, kickers)
- accent-soft: #6E7E55 (sage green — secondary highlights, chips, supporting glow)

- sans: Hanken Grotesk (display + body; preset default sans is fine if unavailable)
- serif: Fraunces (italic-serif accents — taglines, pull-quotes, mission line)
- mono: system (small labels on neumorphic cards)

### Mark

A single sprouting tendril — one curved vine-line that branches into a small network of connected dots (a sensor-mesh "constellation"). Reads as both a growing plant and a node graph. Render as inline SVG in the brand copper, with the dots in sage. Used as the cover centerpiece and as a small corner glyph elsewhere.

### Header badge

- "Internal — founders' pitch"

## Voice

- Tone: Confident, concrete, honest. Builds on competitors (especially Aigen) rather than trashing them. First-person — this is Filippo talking to two people he trusts.
- Density: One argument per scene; one scene per argument. Prose and big numbers over dense bullets. Let single statements breathe on the statement scenes.
- Pacing: Brisk through the setup, slow on the three "burden" scenes (15–17) and the close. Beats should reveal one idea at a time so nothing lands all at once.
- Vocabulary: Plain. When a term is technical (agentic loop, modes of action, GCFI), gloss it in a half-sentence. Assume robotics/AI fluency but zero agriculture background.
- Honesty: Flag open items and unverified figures explicitly. The beachhead crop is genuinely undecided — say so. The Griggs dollar/views figures are his spoken claims, not independent data — frame them that way. Never hide a TBD.
